* Very briefly (3 sentences) tell us why state management is important
=======================================================================

State management is important because it allows you to maintain control throughout the application.
Furthermore state management simplifies the application allowing you to access data from multiple
locations. State management allows you to reuse logic and reproduce different states more easily.






* Very briefly (3 sentences) tell us why state management may not be necessary
=======================================================================

State management may not be necessary for states that are not global, and only pertains
to a single object. Unless the state is difficult to reproduce. Furthermore, state management
should not be considered if implementing it would only make your application more complex.
